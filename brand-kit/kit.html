05 — Color
·
Tokens & usage
§ 05.1 — Two palettes

One system, day and night.

§ 05.1a — Daylight
Daylight. Paper & deep gold.

The default. Long-form reading, print-safe, AA everywhere.

§ 05.1b — Midnight
Midnight. Void & gold.

The brand surface. Signals, title cards, anything that needs to glow.

§ 05.2 — Rule
Ink dominates

80% of any surface is --bg or --bg-2. Accent is punctuation, not language.

§ 05.3 — Rule
Gold is for names

Accent colors the surname, key verbs, and destinations. If everything is gold, nothing is.

§ 05.4 — Rule
Lilac is for data

Secondary accent is reserved for technical signal: tags, code tokens, metadata. Never decorative.

06 — Grid
·
9-col · Rhythm of threes
§ 06.1 — Structure

A grid you can hear.

9
Columns

Always nine. Spans of 3, 6, 9 — the rhythm is divisible three ways. Never 4, 5, 7, or 8.

9
Base unit (px)

All spacing is a multiple of 9: 9, 18, 27, 36, 54, 72, 108. The scale compounds in threes.

ch
Measure

Body measure caps at 54ch. Lede caps at 36ch. Labels cap at 27ch. Read, don't scan.

3px
Rules

No hairlines. Section breaks are heavy — 3px solid or layered double rules. The line is part of the voice.
